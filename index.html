About us

We make games worth coming back to

QuantixLabs is an independent mobile studio focused on crafting games and entertainment apps that respect your time and reward your attention.

  • Player-first design
    Every decision starts with the experience. We don't ship something we wouldn't enjoy ourselves.
  • Clean, honest monetisation
    No dark patterns. No pay-to-win. Games are free to play with optional extras that feel fair.
  • Lightweight & polished
    We obsess over performance. Apps are fast, battery-friendly, and work on a wide range of Android devices.
  • Privacy by default
    We collect only what we need. Your data stays yours.

How we work

From idea to Play Store

A lean, focused process that keeps the player at the centre of every step.

01
Concept

We start with a simple question — is this fun? Rapid prototypes before any polish.

02
Build

Lean development cycles. We ship features, not roadmaps, and iterate quickly based on feedback.

03
Test

Real device testing across a wide range of Android hardware. Performance and battery are non-negotiable.

04
Launch

Released on Google Play with ongoing updates. We stay active — bugs get fixed, content gets added.
